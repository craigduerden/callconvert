Common Questions

How is this different from buying a data list from Cognism or Apollo?
Data providers sell you contacts. The Pipeline Starter gives you contacts plus the ICP logic that tells you which contacts are worth calling, plus the messaging that's been tested in live outbound campaigns. A list without a playbook gets worked once and abandoned. That's why most list purchases fail.
Why 500 prospects and not 2,000?
500 prospects, properly worked through a full sequence, is 8–10 weeks of quality outbound activity for a small team. Buying 2,000 contacts sounds better but guarantees most of them will never be contacted. We'd rather you actually work the list.
What happens if the data is bad?
Every prospect is validated before delivery. If any record is inaccurate — wrong person, wrong role, bad contact details — we replace it. No negotiation. Standard in our retainer, standard here.

No. The Pipeline Starter is complete on its own. The £1,000 credit is there if you want it — most Starter clients use the 90 days to decide. Some never upgrade because their team runs it well in-house. Both outcomes are fine.
